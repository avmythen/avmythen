EDUCATION

Qualification: Master of Engineering (MENG)

Field of Study: Aerospace Engineering

Grade: First-Class Honours

Institution: The University of Sheffield

Length of Programme: 4 Years (September 2017 – June 2021)

I chose to study Aerospace Engineering because of my passion for mathematics, physics and curiosity about how things work – particularly flight and space exploration. However, throughout my studies it became apparent my passion lies deeper within sustainability, and how data, innovation and engineering can make a huge impact on the urgent need to address the climate crisis.

The course was both rigorous and diverse, combining traditional topics such as aerodynamics, propulsion, and lightweight structures with modern focuses like systems integration, autonomous control, and software tools. This gave me a strong foundation in technical problem-solving, computational modelling, and working with complex datasets – skills I now apply directly to my career as a Data Engineer / Business Analyst. Studying through the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic also strengthened my ability to self-learn and adapt. Alongside this, hands-on projects, flight simulators, and research opportunities further developed my technical expertise and deepened my appreciation for the role data plays in solving real-world problems.
